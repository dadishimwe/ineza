Our Story

The story of INEZA Cooperative is one of strength, courage, and community. All the women of INEZA Cooperative are living with HIV/AIDS as a result of genocidal rape and sexual violence. However, rather than focusing on the horrors of the genocide and its aftermath, the women of INEZA Cooperative are a testament to the power of Rwandan women to work toward a better future for themselves, their families, and their nation.

Our Programs

Crafts Program

Our crafts program empowers women to create beautiful handmade items, including baskets, jewelry, and home decor. These crafts are a reflection of Rwandan culture and tradition, and they provide a sustainable source of income for the women.

Clothing Program

Through our clothing program, women learn to design and sew traditional African clothing. These garments are sold locally and internationally, showcasing the talent and creativity of Rwandan women while supporting their livelihoods.

Empowerment Program

Our empowerment program focuses on education, health, and leadership training. We provide workshops and resources to help women build confidence, improve their health, and take on leadership roles in their communities.
